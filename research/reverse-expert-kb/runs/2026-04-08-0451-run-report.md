## New findings
Primary durable split reinforced this run:

```text
accepted
  != replied
  != reconnected
  != consumed
```

Concrete practical reinforcements preserved in the note:
- listener acceptance is weaker than exported-object method entry
- `remoteObjectProxyWithErrorHandler(...)` error visibility is weaker than proving whether failure was listener-rejection, contract-shape mismatch, lifecycle breakage, or later semantic rejection
- invalidation is a stronger end-of-life signal than ordinary interruption-like drift because Apple warns against sending messages from the invalidation handler
- a later healthy-looking connection is reconnection truth, not automatic same-request or same-consumer truth
