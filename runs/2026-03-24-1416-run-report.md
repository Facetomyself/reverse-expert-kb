### Practical content added
1. **IOCP stop-rule sharpening**
   - do not infer consumer order from submit order alone
   - keep completion key and `OVERLAPPED*` ownership distinct
   - separate `PostQueuedCompletionStatus` control packets from I/O-owned packets
   - treat failed dequeue returns with non-NULL `lpOverlapped` as potentially real delivery

2. **Windows thread-pool I/O reminder**
   - `CreateThreadpoolIo` is not enough by itself
   - `StartThreadpoolIo` matters
   - immediately successful operations under `FILE_SKIP_COMPLETION_PORT_ON_SUCCESS` may skip the callback and instead require `CancelThreadpoolIo`
   - absence of callback is not automatically evidence that the consumer is dead or unrelated

3. **libuv ownership split sharpening**
   - preserve the difference between worker-thread `work_cb` ownership and loop-thread `after_work_cb` ownership
   - treat `uv_async_send()` as a distinct continuation boundary rather than flattening it into the same handoff semantics as `uv_queue_work()`
